Why Choose Us?

We offer excellent benefits not found among any other local video production studio. Located in Kittery Point, Maine, we travel all around the Greater Boston Area, the East Coast, and beyond. With Seapoint Studios, your film or video will stand out from the rest.

PRICING

We understand that you may have a limited budget. We offer tiered production prices to accommodate everyone. Once you contact us, we can discuss different options based on your project requirements.

UNIFORMITY

Because of our extensive knowledge in technical standards we're able to shoot and edit multiple cuts if desired. We understand the different requirements needed, from quick-loading websites to DCP projections.

QUALITY

Unless otherwise specified, we shoot all our footage on
high-end equipment at 4K UHD formats or above, with high bitrate codecs. This gives our work a crisper look, even when viewing on lower resolution devices.

SUPPORT

Need a different file format? Want an altered cut for socials or broadcast? We're always available to make revisions to your project. No need to worry about changing your mind!

Feature Films

Features are the biggest service we provide. We carefully consider which screenplays to produce, as it takes a lot of time and budget to create a feature. If you are interested in submitting your scripts, please contact us directly.

Commercial / Broadcast

Our team can help bring small and local businesses to screens everywhere. We can provide your company with videos for television, social media and any other platform.

Post-Production

We offer project based editing. Our skilled editors can put together your video or film, saving you time and money. We are experts in narrative, audio design, color, VFX and much more!
