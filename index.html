Why Choose Us?

We offer excellent benefits not found among any other local video production studio. Located in Kittery Point, Maine, we travel all around the Greater Boston Area, the East Coast, and beyond. With Seapoint Studios, your film project will stand out from the rest.

PRICING

We understand that you may have a limited budget. We offer tiered production prices to accommodate everyone. Once you contact us, we can discuss different options based on your project requirements.

UNIFORMITY

Because of our extensive knowledge in Computer Science, Design, and Cinema, we're able to shoot and edit multiple cuts if desired. We understand the different requirements needed, from websites to projections.

QUALITY

Unless otherwise specified, we shoot all our footage on
high-end equipment at 4K (3840x2160) at 23.97/29.97fps. This gives our work a crisper look, even when viewing on lower resolution devices.

SUPPORT

Need a different file format? Want an altered cut for socials? We'll make reasonable adjustments to your product, up to a year after the shoot. No need to worry about changing your mind!

Short Films

Feature Films

Features are the biggest service we provide. We carefully pick which screenplays to produce, as it takes a lot of time and effort to create a full film. If you are interested, please contact us directly.

Commercial / TV

Our team can help bring small and local businesses to screens everywhere. We can provide your company with videos for television, social media and any other platform.

Post-Production

We offer single-project based editing. Our team of skilled editors can put together your video, saving you time and money. Flat-rates are available and we provide edit revisions at no additional cost.
