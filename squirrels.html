The Squirrels in the Attic

Official Information Page

When public defender Ingrid makes a house call to sex offender Dane Gage’s isolated home, the jarring appearance of a young woman, Kame, changes Ingrid’s focus of her interrogation. As information is revealed and the power dynamics shift between the three of them, one question pushes its way to the surface;
Who is safe and who is not?
