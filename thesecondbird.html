Official Announcement

Seapoint Studios is extremely excited to announce that The Second Bird, a featurette directed by Jacob J. Christiansen, has entered a year-long production with a killer crew, with our first production day already wrapped! This comedy film, broken up narratively by 4 seasons, will explore the struggle of an amateur artist and his inexplicably bad luck. Narratively, this film is inspired by true events, and takes visual inspiration from nature documentaries and land/seascape photography.

We're seeking financial backing from anyone who's able and willing to support local ME & NH artists on this extremely special project. Please sign up below to stay informed about our campaign and production goals!
